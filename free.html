Free Entry (Postal Entry)

You can enter this prize draw for FREE by post. Free entry is available to anyone worldwide and offers the same chance of winning as paid entry.

Important: Free entry is available by post only. No online free entry is offered.

How to Enter for Free

To enter for free, please send a handwritten entry by post including:

  • Your full name
  • Your full postal address
  • Your email address
  • The name of the competition you are entering

Send your entry to the following address:

FREE PRIZE DRAW ENTRY
[Your Company Name]
[Your Postal Address]
United Kingdom

Number of Free Entries

There is no limit to the number of free entries. Each postal entry counts as one valid entry.

Equal Chances

Free postal entries are treated exactly the same as paid entries and have an equal chance of winning. Paid entry is optional and does not increase the chances of winning.

Entry Deadline

All free entries must be received before the closing date stated on the competition page.
